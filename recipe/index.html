RECIPE

how to Make Breaded Pork Chops

Here is a simple recipe for making breaded pork chops easily at your home. Follow the recipe and enjoy the delicious chops

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg ligthly beaten
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1-1/2 cups crushed saltine crackers
  • 6 boneless pork lion chops (1 inch thick)
  • 1/4 cup canola oil

Procedure

  1. In a shallow bowl,combine egg and milk
  2. Place cracker crumbs in another shallow bowl
  3. Dip each pork in chop in egg mixture,then coat with cracker crumbs,patting to make a thick coating
  4. In a large skillet,cook chops in oil for 4-5 minutes on each side or until a thermometer reads 145°.
  5. Let meat stand for 5 minutes before serving.

Your breaded pork chops are ready to serve. Enjoy your breaded pork chops.
